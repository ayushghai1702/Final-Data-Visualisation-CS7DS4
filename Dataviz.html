Suicide Analytics

About 800000 people commit suicide worldwide every year,
of these 17% are residents of India

Union Territories like Puducherry, Andaman & Nicobar have less population so low number of peple committing suicide but the suicidal rate is higher. Among states, Sikkim has the highest suicide rate.

The 3 Ws: Who, Where and Why?

MORE HOUSEWIVES COMMITTING SUICIDE !!! Maximum number of people committing suicides were housewives followed by those in farming/agricultural activities. Overall, one of the major causes of suicide has been reported to be FAMILY PROBLEMS. It is especially higher in the four Southern states. Other major causes of suicide have been mental illness and other prolonged illness. Top means to commit suicide are hanging and consuming poison.

Mental Health Infrastructure Comparison

Other countries with lower suicide rate than India have significantly higher mental health expenditure, more psychiatrists & psychologists working and more mental health hospitals. The data about these normalised parameters of various countries was acquired from the Global Health Observatory data repository of World Health Organisation (WHO).


Government should invest more in mental health expenditure, Train more psychologists & psychiatrists, Build more mental health hospitals to reduce the suicide rate.
